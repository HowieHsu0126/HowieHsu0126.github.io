雅思写作真经总纲
2023-06-24|英语
字数总计: 18.1k|阅读时长: 84 分钟

1 应试

1.1 5 字决：思、准、通、转、稳

1.2 考试内容

1. 写作是雅思考试的第三部分, 在听力、阅读之后, 考试时间为考试日 (通常为周六)上午 11 点至 12点。 2. Task 1 和 Task 2 的分值权重为 3 比 7。 3. Task 2 中议论文 (Argumentation) 出题概率为 80%, 题目有两种形式： 1. 题目中给出一方观点 (反方观点隐含) 1. Do you agree or disagree? 2. To what extent do you agree or disagree? 2. 题目中给出双方观点, 要求分析正反观点后下结论。 1. Some say... Others argue that... What is your opinion? 2. Discuss both views and give your opinion. 3. Express some reasons for both views and give your own opinion. 4. Do you think its advantages outweigh the disadvantages? 5. (题目中表示“优势和劣势”的同义词: positive and negative; pros and cons;, merits and demerits; benefits and drawbacks) 4. Task2 中的报告文 (Report) 出题概率为 20% 1. 题目中没有观点, 只给出一种现象, 让考生分析该现象的 causes 原因、solutions 解决方案、effects 影响等。通常题目标志词为 What are...?

1.3 考试目的

  1. 小作文：调研数据的总结分析技能；
  2. 大作文：逻辑推导论证的写作技能。

1.4 写作流程

  1. 按正常顺序, 迅速写完 Task 1, 再集中全部精力创作 Task 2
  2. Task 2 的写作流程

1.5 写作格式

  1. 文章格式：缩进式（每段开始空 4个宇符的空格）
  2. 数字：十以下数字用英文单词表示
  3. 标点符号
    1. 没有书名号和顿号。雅思写作中用双引号代替书名号，用逗号代替顿号。
    2. 雅思作文中要用 to name just a few 或 and so on 来代替省略号
  4. I, my, we 可以使用。You 最好不要使用, 可用泛指 someone, one 替代。第三人称用 he or she 表达。

1.6 评分标准

1.6.1 Task Response (TR)

要平衡布局, 不能有的段落写7句话, 有的段落写2句话; 结论段要完整、有力或升华, 不能简单重复: 要兼顾讨论正反双方观点; 回答题目中所有问题。

1.6.2 Coherence and Cohesion

  1. 在写作中要有意识地设置每个自然段的中心句。比如: 首段的最后一句、从第二段开始以后的每段首句都应该设置成该段的中心句。
  2. 文章推进始终清晰
  3. 恰当使用逻辑连接词

1.6.3 Lexical Resource

  1. 灵活性：设置同义词替换
  2. 准确性：贴切的、核心的词汇
  3. 不普通的、含义较深刻的词汇：style (文体)和 collocation (搭配)

1.6.4 Grammatical Range and Accuracy

  1. 语法的正确性
  2. 使用多种多样的复合句 (uses a variety of complex structures)。
    • 作文中要写出4种不同的从句, 比如: 一篇作文中至少要写出宾语从句、定语从句、状语从句和主语从句
    • 范文平均每篇 12句话, 2句话是简单句, 2 句话是并列句, 其余8句话是各种从句

1.7 学习方法

  1. 只有先输人正确的信息, 才能够在写作中保证输出的信息是正确的。
    • 平时写文章前我们一定要阅读相关的范文, 大量输入和积累地道的、贴切的英文词汇和句式
  2. 读写互通：平时写任何一篇习作之前, 要花至少半小时阅读学习相关的两三篇高分范文, 吸收范文的思想、例子、地道的词语搭配和句型表达, 而后产生一种创作冲动, 即想要写一篇文章, 既包含这些范文的所有优点, 又融合自己的思想观点。

1.8 考生要掌握的逻辑技能

1.8.1 段落间逻辑

  1. 起: 你的作文中只可能出现一次“首先”, 所以你只需学会一个词：Firstly。
  2. 承: 你的作文中可能出现两次“而且” 表达递进, 所以你需学会两个词：Moreover, Besides
  3. 转: 你的作文中可能出现两次 “但是”, 所以你需学会两个词：However, But
  4. 合: 你的作文中只可能出现一次“总之”, 所以你只需学会一个词：In summary。

1.8.2 句子间逻辑

1.8.2.1 因果

  1. Because + 句子：因为
  2. due to + 名词：由于
  3. So,+ 句子：所以

1.8.2.2 对比

  1. Compared with sth....：与某事相对比
  2. By/In contrast：与之相对比 (用于句首, 表达前后句之间的对比)
  3. On the other hand：另一方面
  4. On the contrary：相反 (反义词: Likewise, 同样地)

1.8.2.3 让步

  1. While + 句子：尽管
  2. Despite +名词：尽管

1.8.2.4 目的

  1. In order to do sth：为了做某事…
  2. thereby doing sth. 以此来⋯
    • 高分表达
    • The government should raise the public's green awareness, thereby creating a more livable environment.

1.8.2.5 举例

  1. For example...：例如

2.2.5 名词动化

  1. Harbour 持有，抱有
    • We should not harbor prejudice or suspicion to cultures we do not understand.
  2. Mirror 反映
    • Many of today's social problems are not new but mirror similar dilemmas from the past.
  3. Hinge 取决于
    • Humanity's ability to achieve international peaces hinges on our ability to find [[common ground]].
  4. Shape 塑造
    • University education should shape the mind and broaden horizons.
  5. Harness 利用
    • By harnessing solar and wind energy, we can help our societies become more "green".
  6. Pool 汇聚
    • Only by pooling their resources can governments hope to overcome global warming.
  7. Mask 掩饰
    • High test scores mask many students' inability to communicate effectivity in a second language.
  8. Bridge 连接
    • Governments must improve social welfare in order to bridge the gap between rich and poor.
  9. Tailor 定制
    • Each country needs to tailor a solution which meets their own particular situation.
  10. Curb 控制
    • In order to curb students' appetite for computer games, parents and schools should come up with healthy alternatives.

2.2.6 拉丁词

  1. Vice versa 反之亦然
    • Language influences culture and vice versa.
  2. De facto 实际上，事实上
    • English is the de facto international language, and people with good English skills can communicate with the world.
  3. Per capita 人均，每人
    • Increased per capita income created for many families the option of living in a rural area and working in a city.
  4. Per se 本身，自身
    • That does not mean that watching television, per se, is problematic.
  5. Status quo 现状
    • Less industrious people are merely content with the status quo.

2.3 高频词组

2.3.1 6 分动词词组

  1. Solve the problem 解决问题
    1. Whoever created this problem should solve it.
    2. Create/pose/cause the problem
    3. Address/fix/tackle/cope with/deal with the problem
  2. Play a role in sth. 在某事上起作用
    1. Universities play a role in training people for the workforce.
    2. Play a critical/key role in sth.
    3. Have an important role to play
  3. Be responsible for sth. 对某事承担责任
    1. The government should be responsible for preserving cultural heritages.
    2. Have a responsibility to do sth.
    3. Do one's part to do sth.
  4. Pay attention to sth. 关注重视某事
    1. People begin to pay attention to the high unemployment.
    2. Pay more attention to sth.
    3. Lay/place emphasis on sth.
    4. Focus on sth.
  5. Make contributions to sth. 为某事做出贡献
    1. Museums make contributions to exhibition, preservation and research of ancient arts.
    2. Make a great contribution to society
    3. Contribute to sth.
  6. Make an effort to do sth. 努力去做某事
    1. Every citizen should make an effort to protect out environment.
    2. Spare no effort to do sth.
    3. Make every effort to do sth. = do one's best to do sth.
  7. Have an effect/influence/impact on sth. 对某事产生影响
    1. The popularity of mobile phones has a great effect on people's daily life.
    2. Have negative/positive effects/impacts on
    3. Have a serious impact on
    4. Have a direct impact on
    5. Have the greatest impact on
  8. Spend time/money (in) doing sth. 花时间（金钱）在某事上
    1. Nowadays, we spend too much time in commuting to work everyday.
    2. Spend time/money on sth.
    3. Spend more time with families
  9. Benefit from sth. 从某事中收益
    1. People can benefit from low-carbon economy.
    2. Be beneficial to sth,
    3. Do harm to sth.
    4. Do good to sth.
    5. Be harmful to/on sth.
  10. Take steps to do sth. 采取措施做某事
    1. The government must take steps to curb the housing price.
    2. Take effective measures to do sth.
    3. Take [[concrete]] actions to do sth.

2.3.2 7分动词词组

  1. Improve people's living standard 提升人们的生活水平
    1. The global trade has improved people's living standard.
  2. Adapt to 适应
    1. University graduates should learn to adjust and adapt to social environment.
  3. Provide sth for sb
    1. Watching TV and playing video games provide entertainment and pleasure for kids.
  4. Rely on
    1. Many regions of the world rely overly on the tourist industry to develop local economy.
  5. Relieve stress / burden
    1. Regular exercise can relive stress and reduce fatigue.
  6. Distinguish between A and B
    1. Many youngsters lack the capacity to distinguish between right and wrong.
  7. Be involved in
    1. Every citizen should be involved in environmental protection.
  8. Be addicted to sth
    1. Now many kids are addicted to playing online games.
  9. Stem from
    1. Generally, most crimes stem from poor education and poverty.
  10. Raise the public's awareness of
    1. The government should raise the public's awareness of conserving water.
  11. Boost economic development 促进经济发展
    1. Modern science and technologies largely boost economic development worldwide.
  12. Keep pace with 并驾齐驱，跟上

3.3 六种复合句

3.3.1 宾语从句

3.3.2 状语从句

3.3.2.1 If

  • Many medications and procedures currently in use would not exist if animal testing was banned.

3.3.2.2 While

  • While the economic development has made our life more comfortable, it has also polarized the society in the distribution of wealth.
  • While traditional buildings might look nice from the outside, the are often not very user-friendly.

3.3.3 定语从句

3.3.3.1 Who 引导，修饰“人”

  • Those who feel that sports stars's salaries are justified might argue that the number of professionals with real talent are very few.
  • Children who enter school at an early age are generally more confident and independent than children who stay at home with their parents.

3.3.3.2 That 引导，修饰“物”

  • Likewise, economics is a fundamental discipline that allows societies to be stable and move forward.
  • Advertisement is an effective marketing tool that almost every company uses.

3.3.3.3 逗号后加 which 引导, 修饰前面的整个句子 (非限制性定语从句)

  • If fuel prices go up, either fewer people will drive or people will drive less, which makes sense for reducing pollution.
  • Demand for various commodities creates a huge market for the local and international businesses, which in turn increases demand for the labour market.
  • Governments should develop and launch a good traffic control system, which can monitor and facilitate traffic flows on major roads for better and safer road usage, reducing the chances of accidents.

3.3.4 主语从句

3.3.4.1 whether 引导的主语从句

  • Whether mobile phones bring more harm than good to us has caused heated debate.
  • Whether the increase of fuel price can solve the world's environmental problem is a controversial issue.
  • Whether universities should provide students with practical skills or academic knowledge is a controversial issue.

3.3.4.2 先行词 it 开始的主语从句

  • It is obvious that we are living in an information age.
  • It is obvious that investing in education is investing in one's future.
  • It is obvious that human activities have the greatest impact on environment.

3.3.4.3 what 引导的主语从句 + not... but... 搭配

  • What governments should do is to create policies to encourage low carbon development.
  • What most companies and workforces need is not robots, but creative people who can contribute ideas.
  • What the poor countries need most is not money, but advanced technologies and talents in some key areas.

3.3.5 同位语从句

  • Nowadays, an increasing number of people are concerned about the phenomenon that our kids are spending more time watching TV.
  • I agree with the view that not only governments but every single citizen should do one's part to fix this problem.
  • The fact that we have different languages demonstrates that we have different cultures.

3.3.6 表语从句

  • This means that change is not always a personal option, but an inescapable fact of life.
  • This is because it is the rich and powerful people in out society who are able to impose changes.
  • Another reason for this is that old buildings, like native language, form a cultural identity and keep a unique record for a country.

3.3.7 经典句式

  1. There are, however, some disadvantages.
    1. However, there may be better ways of tackling this problem.
    2. However, the reverse is also true.
  2. not only... but also...后语法成分对等：People should be encouraged not only to be more physically active but also to adopt a healthier lifestyle in general.
  3. 议论文经典句型, 一句话介绍某观点和论据：Those who believe teenagers are the happiest people cite their lack of responsibilities as a significant factor.
  4. 井列结构和井列句让句子变长：Educating children to understand the need to obey rules and respect others always begins in the home and is widely thought to be the responsibility of parents.
  5. 背记句型 Doing sth. (方法〕 would be a more effective strategy/ method for doing sth. (目的)：Persuading manufacturers and travelers to adopt this new technology would be a more effective strategy for improving air quality, especially in cities.
  6. 一句话说清楚某观点和相关论据, 3个从句嵌套：Some educationalists think it is more effective to educate boys and girls in single-sex schools because they believe this environment reduces distractions and encourages pupils to concentrate on their studies.
  7. 当完全持中立观点时, 强调两者缺一不可：Without the natural talent, continuous training would be neither attractive nor productive, and without the training, the child would not learn how to exploit and develop their talent.
  8. 两个定语从句, 对仗工整, 突出了对比：It often seems that the amount of money they are able to earn in a short time cannot possibly be justified by the amount of work they do.
  9. 一句话交代某观点和相应的论据, 三个从句嵌套：Those who feel that sports stars' salaries are justified might argue that the number of professionals with real talent are very few.
  10. not... but... 在强调自己观点正确的同时批驳反方, 夹叙夹议：This means that change is not always a personal option, but an inescapable fact of life, and we need to constantly adapt to keep pace with it.
  11. 定语从句表达对比含义：Spending time with other children and sharing non-virtual experiences is an important part of a child's development that cannot be provided by a computer.
  12. 学习主语从句; 词汇搭配地道表达：Therefore it is important that children learn at an early age to use the equipment enthusiastically and with confidence as they will need these skills throughout their studies and working lives.
  13. 简简单单首句, 朴实平淡开篇：It is quite common these days for young people in many countries to have a break from studying after graduating from high school.
  14. 表达一系列解决方案：Governments can encourage such action by putting taxes on packaging, such as plastic bags, by providing recycling services and by fining households and shops that do not attempt to recycle their waste.
  15. 让步状语强调优势, 注意 there are many advantages to doing sth：While some people think this may be damaging to a child's development, or to a child's relationship with his or her parents, in fact there are many advantages to having school experience at a young age.

3.3.8 地道表达

  1. thereby doing… 以此来：The government should raise the public's green awareness, thereby creating a more livable environment.
  2. the more.. the more...：The more points of view and various backgrounds we have in politics, the healthier our system and society will be.
  3. not to mention 更别提, 更不必说：Pollution has a negative effect on the health of everyone living in the city, not to mention the damage to the environment.
  4. not only... but also...：Some have argued that watching TV and playing computer games are not only a waste of time, but potentially harmful.
  5. fail to 未能; 否定词 nowhere 在句首倒装：Advertisements and commercials appear everywhere in modern society. Nowhere can you fail to see them.
  6. far from 远非：Our experience of the Web is still far from perfect in many ways.
  7. 双重否定：
    1. It is almost impossible for a person to get a decent job without a higher degree.
    2. It is not uncommon that different persons entertain themselves in different ways.
  8. 强调句型：
    1. It is the government that should be responsible for it.
    2. I personally think that some people do have talents that are probably inherited via their genes.
  9. 恰到好处地使用一些修辞手法：
    1. In a word, in modern society overwork is stealing our leisure time.
    2. There is a growing tendency nowadays for some people to treat animals as living meat and walking fur.
  10. 长短向结合与插入语：如果整篇使用复杂句, 会让读者在阅读时感觉喘不过气来。这时就需要一些换气符号。
    1. It is obvious that... 有时换成 Obviously 更好。
    2. The result is that... 有时换成 As a result 更好。

4.2 初级版本

4.3.1 考题

[!Warning] 这是一篇教育类的高频经典考题, 历年来考过多次, 请认真学习。

4.3.2 写作提纲

  • 分类：教育类
  • 观点：大学应该更注重提供学生工作相关技能
  • 原因 1：对学生有利
    • 大部分学生上大学的目的是将来好找工作
  • 原因 2：对大学有利
    • 工作所需的知识技能更新快，大学重视这些知识的研究本身也能提升进步

4.3.3 正文

4.3.3.1 首段

[!Warning] 首段写作我们要做到无错、清晰、快速。不要花太多时间在首段冥思苦想、宇斟句酌。要把更多的时问和精彩的论证思想留给文章的主体段落。首段要快速完成, 迅速进入写作状态!

  1. 第一句：背景介绍
    1. Nowadays, an increasing number of people are concerned about the purpose of university education.
    2. 还应掌握：
      1. Nowadays, an increasing number of people are concerned about the phenomenon that...
      2. Nowadays, an increasing number of people are concerned about the purposes that university education serves.
      3. Nowadays, more and more students are concerned about what they will learn in the universities.
      4. Universities are one of the important institutions in modern society.
    3. 直接表达观点：
      1. I agree with the view that... Reasons are as follows.
  2. 第 2 句：交代辩论话题
    1. 既然是辩论话题, 必然有正反双方两种观点。正方说这样做合理, 反方说这样做不合理; 正方说优势大, 反方说劣势大。所以我们必须学会的句型就是表达 “是否应该……”;“是这样…还是应该那样………”; 或者直接写 “一些人认为⋯…; 另一些人认为⋯。”这三种写法考生都应该熟练掌握, 考场上任选其一。
    2. “是否应该...”
      • However, whether universities should mainly provide job-related skills to graduates is a controversial issue.
    3. “是这样... 还是应该那样...”
      • However, whether universities should mainly provide job-related skills or pure knowledge to graduates has caused a heated debate.
      • However, what kind of knowledge and skills universities should mainly provide to graduates is a controversial issue.
    4. “一些人认为...；另一些人认为...”
      • Some people state that universities should focus on giving access to knowledge; others believe that the knowledge and skills needed in the workplace are more important for university graduates.
  3. 第三句：交代你自己的写作观点
    1. 雅思考官的一些高分范文中在文章最后一段才表明观点, 这对广大中国雅思考生来说是难以模仿的。这要求文章说理、例证和让步环环相扣, 最后水到渠成得出结论, 阅读者被潜移默化, 赞同最后的观点。而广大考生模仿的此类文章, 我读起来都属于前中部分比较迷花, 最后恍然大悟, 其实, 准确地说最后不是恍然大悟, 而是恍然有所悟。于是我会批注: The position is not clear throughout.
    2. 既然我们的英语作文很难老谋深算地谋篇布局, 处处伏笔, 前后呼应, 那么我们应该在首段明确地提出写作观点。这样做其实给考官一点心理暗示, 帮助他们在接下来读到费解句子的时候, 能通过前面你的鲜明立场体会出你想表达的思想或道理, 让他们在阅读中更加地轻松。表达写作观点学习下面几种就足够了:
      1. In my view,
      2. My view is that... (表语从句)
      3. I agree with the view that ... (同位语从句), for the following reasons.
    3. 如果上一句你写的是“一些人认为⋯; 另一些人认为⋯”, 接下来这句也可以更简单地写成: I mainly agree with the former (前者) / latter (后者) view for the following reasons. 不要写出 50%对 50% 的完全中立观点, 如: There is value in both sides. 这会给接下来的写作带来困难, 很容易导致立场不清晰。就好像别人问你:“他的观点是... 而她的观点是... 你的观点是什么?”而你回答:“我的观点是他们的观点都有道理。”只有超级高手才敢一开始使用此等无赖招数, 因为他们能在接下来的行文中化腐朽为神奇。
    4. My view is that job-related knowledge and skills should be the main focus of universities.

4.3.3.2 第二段

[!Warning] 这一段的写作目的是以理服人。用 it is clear that... 这个主语从句写出你认为最重要、最明显的论据。那也是第一个直接进入脑海中的 idea。 that 后写一个完整的句子, 内容应该是一个众所周知的事实。

  1. 第 1 句：本段中心句
    1. Firstly, it is obvious that for most students, the purpose of receiving university education is to obtain practical knowledge and skills which will enable them to become qualified employees in the job market in the future.
    2. 还可掌握：
      1. Firstly, it is clear / obvious / true that...
      2. Firstly, there is on doubt that...
      3. One reason for this is that...
  2. 第 2 句：对中心句进行扩展、解释、说明。
    1. 很多考生的问题是:“写完中心句了, 下一句不知道写什么。”所以该句是 15句框架模板中最难写的一句。我给大家了个句型选择, 注意这了个句型暗示了在考场上的思维方向。
    2. This is because... 这是因为（想上一句话的原因）
      1. This is because most of them, after graduation, will enter the workplace, earn a living and support their own families.
    3. This means that... 这意味着（想上一句话的结果）
      1. This means that the courses focusing on jobs will become the most welcomed subjects which the majority of students will select.
    4. If... not...（反证中心句）
      1. If universities do not pay enough attention to such skills, most graduates will not be able to find satisfactory positions in the workplace, leading to a higher unemployment rate.
  3. 第 3 句：重申写作观点。其实是对第一段最后一句话, 也就是你的写作观点进行改写表达。
    1. So universities, as service providers, have to give top priority to these customers' real needs.
    2. 还推荐一个固定句型来写本向, 让写作更简化: Therefore, it is necessary/crucial/important for sb. to do sth.
      • Therefore, it is crucial for universities to give top priority to the students' real needs.

4.3.3.3 第三段

  1. 第 1 句：首句仍是该段中心句，是你绞尽脑汁想出的第二个原因论据。
    1. Moreover, focusing on the job-related skills can benefit the university itself.
    2. Moreover = In addition = Besides
  2. 第 2 句：中国考生的作文中有一个很大的问题: 说理太多, 缺乏具体例证。其实考官读完这么多相似观点的文章后, 感觉道理都是大同小异, 但印象最深的一定是某一篇习作中有一个具体而贴切的例子。所以, 我们必须强迫自己写: For example, ... 这种方法也回应题目中的要求:“include relevant examples from your own knowledge or experience" 。所以该段第 2 句就是举例。
    1. For example, the sci-fi blockbuster "Avatar" has marked a milestone in the filmmaking industry and drawn massive interest in 3D technology.
  3. 第 3 句：上一句己经举出了《阿凡达》和 3D 的例子, 为什么我们要提这个例子呢? 接下来的这句话就是要写出我们举这个例子的原因, 并证明本段首句的正确性。
    1. If a university can quickly respond, and consolidate teaching resources to offer courses about 3D, not only will its graduates be more competitive in the job market, but the university itself will keep pace with the technological development and move forward.
    2. 这里没有固定句式要用, 可随意发挥, 要写出例子和中心句之间的联系。
      1. 当然还可以再举一例: Another example is sth. 然后解释说明。
      2. 如在考场上实在想不出好的例子或并列例子, 该段从第 2 句开始参考第二段的写作框架, 再讲一次道理。
  4. 仿写：For example, the AI-powered model "ChatGPT" has marked a milestone in the tech industry and drawn massive interest in AI research and applications. If a university can quickly respond, and consolidate teaching resources to offer courses about AI, not only will its graduates be more competitive in the job market, but the university itself will keep pace with the technological development and move forward.

[!Warning] 写让步段落的两大好处: 1. 大多数考生绞尽脑汁也想不出第三个原因论据。那就不要绞了, 因为上一段已经绞过了, 现在再想是在榨汁了。我们何不换一个角度, 想一想支持反方的一个原因或例子呢, 那会容易得多。 2. 兼顾了反方观点, 不会出现评分标准中因为 partly address the task 而丢分的情况。

  1. 第 1 句：我们要学习三种句式来评论反方观点, 真实考试中视情况任选一种。
    1. This is not to say that ... not ... 这并不是说（反方观点完全没有道理）
      1. That is not to say that universities should not pay any attention to developing pure science and knowledge.
    2. It is also true that... （反方观点中的某些方面）也是事实
      1. It is also true that universities should develop pure science and knowledge.
    3. Those who harbor the view that ... believe that ... 那些持有（反方观点）的人们认为... (这个句型中包含了定语从句、同位语从句和宾语从句)
      1. Those who harbor the view that the main function of universities should be to give access to the knowledge believe that the theoretic knowledge is the foundation of any practical skill.
    4. 3 句式中从句嵌套复杂, 并不建议考生勉强背通使用。因为如果整篇文章句型复杂度不高, 即使这句话语法能完全正确, 考官也能看出背诵痕迹。在讨论优势和劣势的作文中, 我们可以直接使用下列句型:
      1. This is not to say, however, that we should neglect the problems it has caused.
      2. But to acknowledge only the positive impact would be short sighted.
  2. 第 2 句：想反方观点的论据, 或在特殊、极端情况下的一些合理性。进一步说明该段第一句话。
    1. 可选用 Of cause 或 Indeed 开始本句。
    2. There are, of course, students interested in knowledge for its own sake, and they will later become scientists and scholars who will make greater contributions to our society.
  3. 第 3 句：让步后最终转折。表达“即便如此, 反方观点在大多数情况下还是不可行的”或“即使存在上面提到的负面, 正面影响仍是大于负面的”
    1. But unfortunately these people are few and far between.
    2. 可选用 But, However, Yet 等词开始本句。
    3. 该段为文章次要信息, 可点到为止, 不可花费过多时间和笔墨纠缠。实在不知如何反驳了, 可用无赖写法:“(虽然反方观点也有道理) 但正方观点仍然更有道理。”
      1. But still, job-related skills are more important for most students.
      2. But still, the [[cons]] are far greater.
    4. 其实本句还可以不用反驳, 再写反方第二论据, 或针对第一论据的拓展、支持、举例。不过这样处理后在结论段要表达相对中立的观点态度。

4.3.3.5 结尾段

[!Warning] 结论段重申写作观点。

  1. 第 1 句：这句话其实是针对首段最后一句话改写。
    1. In summary / conclusion, 总之
    2. In summary, the major function of universities should be providing most students jot-related skills, guaranteeing a high rate of employment, and thus helping the society function better.
    3. 通常, 考官读完一篇作文后印象最深的会是文章中的例子和结尾段。所以该句不是简单的改写, 而应是递进或强调。可用强调句型: It is sth. that...
  2. 第 2 句：照顾反方观点。表达“同时, 我们不能忽视反方观点的价值, 或低估某事的负面影响”。
    1. Meanwhile, 同时
    2. Meanwhile, we should not ignore the other important roles universities are undertaking.
    3. 雅思大作文中木段最好不要由一句话单独构成, 会显得你因为时间不够而草草收兵, 虎头蛇尾, 结论不够完整。当然, 除非你是超级高手, 可以用一句话高度概括全文中心, 并彰显文章思想价值。
    4. 模板句式: Meanwhile, we should not ignore /neglect /overlook /underestimate sth.'s negative effects.
  3. 第 3 句：其实木段有了上面两句话已经完整了。如果要冲刺高分, 我们要再加一句话, 给文章面一个有力的句号, 或提升文章的思想境界, 立意升华。
    1. By doing so we can ensure that... 由此可知
    2. Only by doing so can universities maintain dynamic and sustainable development.
    3. 官方 6 分评分标准中提到:“The conclusions may become unclear or repetitive” 所以要考 7 分, 结论段不能简单重复改写, 必须是有力的总结或观点升华。如果没有升华的思想, 就喊口号:
      1. By doing so we can ensure that...
      2. Only by doing so can we make sure that... (only 在句首注意倒装)
      3. 如果觉得口号喊起来很勉强, 那就果断放弃该句写作, 以免画蛇添足。

4.4 15句高级作文写作分折

  2. 分类：社会类-儿童

  4. 提纲：

    3. My view is that too much time spent on it surely has negative impacts on kids and parents should filter out the inappropriate programs and channels for them.
    4. First, it is obvious that, with the rapid development of science and technology, TV shows are designed so vivid and attractive that not only kids but even adults are much easier to get addicted to them.
    5. This means that when children spend more time sitting in front of screen, they are isolated from the real world. They do not have enough time to socialize or to engage in outdoor activities which are crucial for their for their physical growth.
    6. Also, their eyesight will be weakened by staring at the screen for too long.

  • 解题思路: 一般按图例分类描写, 说明极值和趋势, 描述对比结果。

[!Note] 题目

These two bar graphs illustrates the changes of USA marriage and divorce rates between 1970 and 2000, and the marital status of adult Americans in two of the years.

According to the first graph, there were 2.5m marriages in both 1970 and 1980, and then decreased gradually during the next two decades, reaching to 2m in 2000. By comparison, the number of divorces first increased from 1m in 1970 to around 1.4m in 1980 and then slowly declined back to 1m in 2000.

As can be seen from the second bar graph, the percentage of married American adults dropped by more than 10%, from 70% in 1970 to less than 60% in 2000. Meanwhile, the percentage of adult American who were never married increased from about 15% in 1970 to 20% in 2000, and despite still less than 10%, the proportion of divorced adults significantly went up from 1970 to 2000. Moreover, the percentage of the widowed in 1970 and 2000 accounted for less than 10% respectively, and showed a slight drop.

Overall, it is clear that marital status of adult Americans in 2000 was not as optimistic as that of three decades ago.

[!Summary] 点评 - 学习固定结构。 - 体会三大核心句型在范文中的运用, 表达了极值和趋势。 - 切忌没有结论。不要忽视范文最后一句话, 这句话体现了你真正看到了这两幅图表的价值。这些纷繁的数字都在告诉我们一个信息: 美国人的婚姻状态没有三十年前那样乐观了!

5.3.2 表格

  • 特点: 数字较多, 规律不明显。
  • 解题思路: 需要从纵向和横向双向对此总结, 描述最大值和最小值。中间数宇可粗略交代。描述对比结果。

[!Note] 题目

The table illustrates percentage of consumer spending in five different countries (Ireland, Italy, Spain, Sweden and Turkey) in 2002. The consumer expenditures are grouped into three categories.

纵向对比

（从最大的类开始）According to the table, all people in these five nations spent most on food, drink and tobacco （最大值）. They accounted for 32.14% of consumer expenditure in Turkey, which was the highest figure among the five countries, followed by Ireland and Spain. And the lowest proportion, 15.77%, belonged to Sweden.（最大值、最小值）

（第二大类）Clothing and footwear made up the second important consumer spending. Compared with Italian people who spent 9%, the highest percentage on this category, Swede spent the least, 5.40%.（最大值、最小值）

（最小类）In terms of the last group -- leisure and education, on which people in five different countries generally spent less, the percentage in Turkey was comparatively higher, reaching to 4.35%, while that in Spain represented the lowest one: 1.98%.（最大值、最小值）

横向对比

Overall, it is clear that consumer expenditure on three categories in Turkey which was about 44% in total provided the largest proportion. People in Sweden, however, spend the least percentage of expense, about 24% on these basic living needs.

[!Summary] 总结 - 注意范文中的同义词替换手法。 - 注意每一类中的最大值和最小值必须描写, 其他数据可忽略。 - 体会最后一段的结论。

[!Note] 线形图 - 特点: 单曲线和多曲线, 趋势明显 - 解题思路: 各曲线起点、终点. 拐点数值, 变化趋势。两曲线交点说明。描述对比结果。

[!Note] 题目

These graphs illustrate the change of people's eating habits between 1970 and 2000.

According to the four pie charts, the percentage of the family's food budget spent on restaurant meals steadily increased during this period. Just 10 percent of the food budget was spent on restaurant meals in 1970, and 15 percent in 1980. That percentage more than doubled in 1990, to 35 percent, and rose again in 2000 to 50 percent.

As can be seen from the following line graph, families ate the same number of meals at fast food and sit-down restaurants, 20 meals in 1970. In 1980, families ate slightly more frequently at sit-down restaurants. However, at the end of 1980's, the popularity of the fast food caught up with that of the sit-down restaurants, and families ate about 40 meals at both fast food and sit-down restaurants per year. From then on, the number fast food meals increased much more dramatically to 90 meals in 2000, nearly doubling the number of meals they ate at sit-down restaurants.

Overall, it is clear that people spent more and more money on restaurant meals and fast food became an important option when families chose to eat out.

[!Summary] 总结 - 注意结论的全面性和准确性。 - 多个饼图出现要对比。 - 两条曲线交叉, 要描述 A 线在某个时问点超越了 B 线, 用动词: surpass, take over 或 catch up with

8.1 写作逻辑框架

Report (报告)类作文题目中通常包含两个问题。最常见的是题目给出一种当今社会现象, 要考生分析该现象的产生原因, 并闸述影响或提出解决方案。

8.1.1 谋篇布局

行文重点在解释原因。因为原因清晰了, 解决方法就是对症下药。雅思考官也明白考生个人对一些社会问题的解决无能为力, 主要锻炼大家认识问题、分析问题的能力。所以在文章结构上要 2 个自然段解释原因, 1 个自然段描述影响后果或解决方案。

8.1.2 万能原因

既然题目是当今社会现象, 也即是过去不常见的。那么我们就设计出作文第二段的写作框架: In the past, 是那样的。This is no longer always the case. 由于…现在是这样了。”而现在和过去的许多变化都是由于新技术 (new technology)、城市化 (urbanization)、全球化 (globalization)、工作生活节奏加快 (fast-paced work and life style) 等因素导致的。

8.1.3 万能影响和解决之道

影响可从个人、家庭和社会三个层面阐述, 也可分为好、坏两方面写作。而所有社会问题的解决之道都是: 政府有责任; 相关团体做出贡献: 公民个人提升意识、积极参与。

8.1.4 框架

因为是 Report 类作文, 回答完题目中的所有问题即可, 可以不用写第5段。如果担心宇数不够, 可快速写出模板化的第5段。
